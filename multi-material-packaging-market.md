<p><p>The multi-material packaging market is characterized by various polymers that enhance packaging performance. Polyethylene (PE) is flexible and moisture-resistant, ideal for films and pouches. Polypropylene (PP) offers strength and clarity, making it suitable for rigid containers. Polyamide (PA) provides excellent barrier properties, especially for food packaging. EVOH is valued for its superior oxygen barrier, extending shelf life. Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET) is widely used for bottles due to its recyclability and durability, making it popular in beverage packaging.</p></p>
