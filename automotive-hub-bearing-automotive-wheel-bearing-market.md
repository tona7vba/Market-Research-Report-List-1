<p><p>The automotive hub bearing market comprises three main types: Generation 1 (Gen. 1), Generation 2 (Gen. 2), and Generation 3 (Gen. 3) bearings. Gen. 1 bearings are traditional designs, often using separate components for the inner and outer races. Gen. 2 bearings integrate the races into a single unit, offering improved durability and performance. Gen. 3 bearings further enhance these features with advanced materials and design, providing greater load capacity, reduced weight, and enhanced sealing for improved overall efficiency and longevity.</p></p>
