<p><p>Industrial pleated filtering systems are categorized based on their Minimum Efficiency Reporting Value (MERV) ratings, which indicate their effectiveness in trapping airborne particles. The MERV 10 and below segment is suitable for basic filtration needs, capturing larger particles. MERV 12 to 14 filters provide a higher level of efficiency, effectively reducing allergens and smaller particles. MERV 14 and above filters are designed for critical applications, offering superior performance in removing fine dust, bacteria, and viruses, ensuring enhanced air quality in industrial environments.</p></p>
