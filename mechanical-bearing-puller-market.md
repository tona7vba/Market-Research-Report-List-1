<p><p>The mechanical bearing puller market is categorized into two main types: two-arm and three-arm pullers, with additional options classified as "other." Two-arm pullers are designed for simpler applications where space is limited, effectively removing bearings from shafts. Three-arm pullers provide more stability and are ideal for larger or more stubborn bearings, distributing force evenly. Other types may include specialty pullers, such as hydraulic or compact versions, tailored for specific industrial needs, enhancing versatility in bearing removal processes.</p></p>
