<p><ul><li>Frame Bindings</li><li>Tech Bindings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ski touring bindings are essential for backcountry skiing, categorized mainly into frame bindings and tech bindings. Frame bindings offer a robust platform, enabling downhill performance akin to alpine skiing, making them popular among beginners. In contrast, tech bindings are lighter, featuring pins that attach to compatible boots, providing superior uphill efficiency and agility for avid touring enthusiasts. This distinction influences market segmentation, with frame bindings appealing to those prioritizing versatility, while tech bindings attract dedicated backcountry explorers seeking minimal weight.</p></p>
