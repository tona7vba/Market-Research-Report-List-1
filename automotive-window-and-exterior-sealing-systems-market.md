<p><ul><li>EPDM Sealing Systems</li><li>TPE/TPO Sealing Systems</li><li>PVC Sealing Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive window and exterior sealing systems are essential for vehicle integrity, providing weatherproofing, noise reduction, and structural support. EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) sealing systems are widely used for their durability, UV resistance, and temperature tolerance. TPE/TPO (Thermoplastic Elastomer/Olefin) systems offer flexibility, recyclability, and lightweight properties, ideal for modern vehicles. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) sealing systems are cost-effective and provide good insulation but are less durable compared to EPDM and TPE/TPO. Each type caters to specific automotive needs, balancing performance, cost, and environmental considerations.</p></p>
