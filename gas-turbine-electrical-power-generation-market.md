<p><strong>The Gas Turbine Electrical Power Generation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Cycle Gas Turbine (OCGT)</li><li>Combined Cycle Gas Turbine (CCGT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gas turbine electrical power generation comprises two main market types: Open Cycle Gas Turbine (OCGT) and Combined Cycle Gas Turbine (CCGT). OCGT utilizes a simple cycle system where compressed air is mixed with fuel and ignited, producing electricity quickly, ideal for peak demand periods. In contrast, CCGT combines two cycles; it uses both gas and steam turbines, enhancing efficiency by utilizing waste heat to generate additional power, making it suitable for baseload and flexible energy generation.</p></p>
