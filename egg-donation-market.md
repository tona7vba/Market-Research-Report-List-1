<p><p>The egg donation market comprises two primary types: fresh egg and frozen egg markets. Fresh egg donation involves the retrieval of eggs from donors and immediate use in assisted reproductive technologies, often resulting in higher success rates due to optimal egg viability. In contrast, the frozen egg market allows for the cryopreservation of eggs for later use, providing flexibility for both donors and recipients. This method facilitates easier logistics and broader access to donor eggs, accommodating varying needs in fertility treatments.</p></p>
