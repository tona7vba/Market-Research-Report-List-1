<p><p>Intravascular Ultrasound (IVUS) is a minimally invasive imaging technique used during cardiovascular procedures to visualize the interior of blood vessels in real-time. By employing high-frequency ultrasound waves, IVUS provides detailed cross-sectional images of the arterial walls, enabling clinicians to assess plaque composition, vessel diameter, and the overall condition of coronary arteries. This technology plays a significant role in guiding interventional procedures, reducing complications, and improving patient outcomes.</p><p>The IVUS market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing prevalence of cardiovascular diseases, technological advancements in imaging systems, and rising awareness about the benefits of minimally invasive procedures. Additionally, the growing geriatric population and the need for accurate diagnostic tools in vascular diseases are further propelling the demand for IVUS devices. Recent trends indicate a shift toward catheter-based IVUS systems, enhanced imaging capabilities, and integration with other diagnostic modalities such as Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT). The Intravascular Ultrasound (IVUS) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.9% during the forecast period, reflecting the expanding applications and innovations in this critical area of healthcare. This robust growth underscores the essential role of IVUS in contemporary cardiovascular diagnostics and treatment.</p></p>
