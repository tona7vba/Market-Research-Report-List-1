<p><p>The passenger car transmission fluids market comprises two main types: Manual Transmission Fluids (MTFs) and Automatic Transmission Fluids (ATFs). MTFs are designed for manual gearboxes, offering lubrication and protection against wear while ensuring smooth gear shifts. In contrast, ATFs are formulated for automatic transmissions, providing necessary hydraulic pressure, friction characteristics, and thermal stability for optimal performance. Both types are essential for enhancing vehicle efficiency and extending the lifespan of transmission systems, catering to distinct vehicle requirements.</p></p>
