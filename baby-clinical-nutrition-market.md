<p><strong>The Baby Clinical Nutrition Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oral administration</li><li>Enteral administration</li><li>Intravenous administration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Baby Clinical Nutrition Market comprises three main types of administration: oral, enteral, and intravenous. Oral administration involves feeding infants with specialized formulas or supplements directly, typically suitable for those who can feed normally. Enteral administration involves delivering nutrition through a feeding tube, ideal for infants with swallowing difficulties or medical conditions. Intravenous administration provides nutrients directly into the bloodstream, used for critically ill infants or those unable to tolerate other forms of feeding, ensuring immediate nutrient absorption.</p></p>
