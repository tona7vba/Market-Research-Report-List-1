<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Metal Casting in Automotive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity Casting</li><li>High-Pressure Die Casting (HPDC)</li><li>Low-Pressure Die Casting (LPDC)</li><li>Sand Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal casting in the automotive market involves various techniques to produce vehicle components. Gravity casting uses gravitational force to fill molds, ideal for larger, complex parts. High-pressure die casting (HPDC) injects molten metal under high pressure for precise, high-volume production of small, intricate components. Low-pressure die casting (LPDC) employs low-pressure methods, improving surface finish and reducing defects, suitable for high-value parts. Sand casting is a traditional technique utilizing sand molds, offering flexibility for custom designs and low production volumes, especially for prototyping.</p></p>
