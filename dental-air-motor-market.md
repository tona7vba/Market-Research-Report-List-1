<p><strong>The Dental Air Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Spray</li><li>External Spray</li><li>Without Spray</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental air motor market is categorized into three types based on spray application. Internal spray motors integrate spray directly within the handpiece, providing optimal cooling at the working site for procedures requiring high-speed drilling. External spray motors deliver cooling externally, offering control over the spray direction and minimizing aerosol production. Motors without spray operate solely on air pressure and are suitable for tasks that don't generate significant heat, ensuring efficiency and simplicity in dental procedures.</p></p>
