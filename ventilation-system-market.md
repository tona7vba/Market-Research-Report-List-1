<p><p>The ventilation system market features three primary types: wall-mount, ceiling-mount, and cabinet-mount systems. Wall-mount types are typically installed on vertical surfaces, providing efficient airflow in smaller spaces like kitchens or bathrooms. Ceiling-mount systems are ideal for larger areas, promoting even air distribution and often used in commercial settings. Cabinet-mount units are designed for enclosed spaces and are commonly utilized in residential applications to ensure effective ventilation while maintaining a compact footprint, enhancing indoor air quality and comfort.</p></p>
