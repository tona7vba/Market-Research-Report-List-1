<p><p>The AI chips market comprises various types designed for specific tasks. GPUs (Graphics Processing Units) excel in parallel processing, making them ideal for deep learning applications. ASICs (Application-Specific Integrated Circuits) are tailored for specific tasks, offering high efficiency but limited flexibility. FPGAs (Field-Programmable Gate Arrays) can be reconfigured for different applications, providing a balance between performance and versatility. CPUs (Central Processing Units) are general-purpose processors that handle a wide range of tasks but may lack the specialized efficiency of other chip types in AI workloads.</p></p>
