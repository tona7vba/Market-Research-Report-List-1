<p><p>The cylindrical robot market can be categorized into two main types based on degrees of freedom: three-degree of freedom (3-DOF) and multi-degree of freedom (multi-DOF) robots. 3-DOF robots typically consist of rotational and linear movements, making them suitable for simple tasks like pick-and-place operations. In contrast, multi-DOF robots can perform complex motions and are designed for intricate manufacturing processes, enabling higher versatility and adaptability in various applications across industries such as automotive, electronics, and medical.</p></p>
