<p><p>The RF power detectors market is primarily categorized into RMS (Root Mean Square) Power Detectors and Non-RMS Power Detectors. RMS Power Detectors provide accurate measurements of power levels by accounting for varying signal amplitudes, making them ideal for complex signals. In contrast, Non-RMS Power Detectors, such as average power detectors, offer simpler measurements usually suited for consistent signals. These types cater to diverse applications in telecommunications, aerospace, and industrial sectors, reflecting the growing demand for efficient power monitoring technologies.</p></p>
