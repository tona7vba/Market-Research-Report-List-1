<p><p>Mobile lifting boat hoists are essential for handling boats in marinas and shipyards. They come in two main types: self-propelled and towed hoists. Self-propelled boat hoists are equipped with their own power source, allowing for easy maneuverability without requiring additional vehicles. In contrast, towed boat hoists rely on a towing vehicle for movement, making them more cost-effective but less versatile. Both types are designed to lift and transport boats safely, catering to varying operational needs within the marine industry.</p></p>
