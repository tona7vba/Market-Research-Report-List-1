<p><p>The Fixed Multi Gas Detector market comprises several technologies. Catalytic combustion sensors detect flammable gases through a reaction that generates heat. Electrochemical (echem) sensors measure gas concentration via chemical reactions, ideal for toxic gases. Infrared (IR) detectors utilize light absorption to identify gas presence, effective for hydrocarbons and CO2. Photoionization detectors (PID) measure gas ionization by ultraviolet light, making them suitable for detecting volatile organic compounds. Each type caters to diverse applications in safety and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
