<p><p>Aluminum foil laminating machines are categorized based on their lamination processes. Solventless laminating machines use adhesives without solvents, offering eco-friendly solutions and advanced adhesive technology. Dry laminating machines utilize heat and pressure to bond layers without liquid adhesives, promoting faster processes. Dry or wet dual-use laminating machines provide flexibility, allowing users to choose between dry and aqueous laminating methods. Each type serves distinct applications in packaging, food preservation, and product protection, catering to varying industry needs and sustainability goals.</p></p>
