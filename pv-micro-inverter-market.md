<p><p>A PV Micro Inverter is a device used in solar energy systems that converts direct current (DC) electricity generated by a single solar panel into alternating current (AC) electricity. This technology improves overall system efficiency, especially in situations where panels may be shaded or face different orientations.</p><p>The PV Micro Inverter Market is witnessing significant growth, driven by the increasing adoption of renewable energy systems and advancements in solar technology.
