<p><p>Electronic Volume Correctors (EVC) are devices used in the gas and water metering industries to ensure accurate measurement by compensating for temperature and pressure variations. The market for EVC is segmented into single-channel and double-channel types. Single-channel EVCs measure flow in one direction, making them suitable for simpler applications. In contrast, double-channel EVCs can process measurements from two separate channels, allowing for more complex applications and enhanced accuracy, making them ideal for large-scale industrial uses where precise data is critical.</p></p>
