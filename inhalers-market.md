<p><strong>The Inhalers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Metered Dose Inhalers</li><li>Dry Powder Inhalers</li><li>Nasal Inhalers</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inhalers market encompasses various types designed to deliver medication effectively. Metered Dose Inhalers (MDIs) release a specific dose of aerosolized medication with each actuation, ideal for quick relief. Dry Powder Inhalers (DPIs) require patients to inhale deeply to activate and disperse powdered medication. Nasal Inhalers administer drugs through the nasal passage for localized or systemic effects, providing an alternative route. Other inhalers include nebulizers and soft mist inhalers, broadening options for patient needs and preferences in respiratory care.</p></p>
