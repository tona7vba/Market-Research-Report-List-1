<p><p>The symmetric supercapacitor market is primarily categorized into double-layer capacitors and pseudocapacitors. Double-layer capacitors store energy through the electrostatic separation of charges at the electrolyte and electrode interface, offering high power density and rapid charge/discharge cycles. Pseudocapacitors, on the other hand, utilize fast electrochemical processes for energy storage, enhancing energy density and extending lifespan. Both types cater to diverse applications in electric vehicles, consumer electronics, and renewable energy systems, providing efficient energy solutions with distinct characteristics.</p></p>
