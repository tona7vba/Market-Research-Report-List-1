<p><p>The heat and moisture exchanger market includes two primary types: straight style and elbow style. Straight style exchangers are characterized by a linear design that facilitates efficient airflow and moisture transfer, making them ideal for applications requiring maximum effectiveness. In contrast, elbow style exchangers feature bends in their structure, which can save space and adapt to various installations, though they may have slightly reduced efficiency. Both types cater to specific needs in HVAC systems, ensuring optimal indoor air quality and comfort.</p></p>
