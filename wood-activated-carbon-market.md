<p><p>The wood activated carbon market can be segmented into two primary types: powdered and granular. Powdered activated carbon (PAC) has a fine particle size, enhancing its surface area, making it ideal for applications requiring quick adsorption, such as water treatment and air purification. Granular activated carbon (GAC), with larger particles, provides effective adsorption over longer periods, suitable for applications like industrial processes and odor control. Both types leverage the unique properties of wood carbon for diverse environmental and industrial solutions.</p></p>
