<p><p>The Corrugated High Density Polyethylene (HDPE) Pipe market consists of two main types: Single-Wall Corrugated HDPE Pipe and Double Wall Corrugated HDPE Pipe. Single-Wall pipes, characterized by a lightweight design and flexibility, are commonly used for drainage and stormwater applications. In contrast, Double Wall pipes feature an inner smooth layer for flow efficiency and an outer corrugated layer for enhanced strength and rigidity, making them suitable for more demanding applications like sewer systems and underground utilities.</p></p>
