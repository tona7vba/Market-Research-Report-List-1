<p><p>The ducted fume hoods market is segmented into two primary types based on the materials used: antistatic and non-antistatic. Antistatic materials are designed to prevent the buildup of static electricity, making them ideal for environments dealing with flammable or sensitive substances. Non-antistatic materials, while equally effective in fume containment, do not offer the same protection against static discharge. The choice between these materials is driven by specific laboratory needs, safety regulations, and the nature of the chemicals handled.</p></p>
