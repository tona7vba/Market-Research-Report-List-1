In contrast, whitening strips consist of thin, flexible plastic coated with a peroxide-based whitening gel, providing a more concentrated treatment that penetrates deeper for more noticeable results. Both types cater to consumers seeking brighter smiles through accessible and user-friendly solutions.</p></p>
