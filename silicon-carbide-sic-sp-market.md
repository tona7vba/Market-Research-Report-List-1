<p><ul><li>Black SiC</li><li>Green SiC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Silicon Carbide (SiC) market is primarily divided into two types: Black SiC and Green SiC. Black SiC is produced through the carbothermal reduction of quartz sand and petroleum coke, making it suitable for abrasive applications. It offers high hardness and thermal conductivity. In contrast, Green SiC is created by a different method and is purer, making it ideal for advanced applications such as high-performance ceramics and semiconductor devices. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, emphasizing their unique properties.</p></p>
