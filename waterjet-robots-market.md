<p><p>The waterjet robots market comprises three main types: Continuous Jet, Pulse Jet, and Cavitation Jet. Continuous Jet systems deliver a steady stream of water at high pressure, ideal for cutting and material processing. Pulse Jet systems generate intermittent bursts of water, providing controlled impact for more precise applications. Cavitation Jet technology utilizes the explosive formation and collapse of bubbles in a liquid, achieving powerful cleaning and cutting effects. Each type serves distinct industrial applications, enhancing efficiency and versatility in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
