<p><strong>The Pool Lifts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electric</li><li>Hydraulic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pool lifts market features two main types: electric and hydraulic. Electric pool lifts utilize batteries or power sources to operate, providing easy access for users through push-button controls. They are often preferred for their convenience and minimal physical effort required. Conversely, hydraulic pool lifts operate using hydraulic systems, relying on fluid pressure to move the lift. This type is known for its robustness and can support heavier users, making it suitable for various aquatic environments, including public pools.</p></p>
