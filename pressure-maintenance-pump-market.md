<p><p>The pressure-maintenance pump market includes various types of pumps designed to ensure consistent pressure in fluid systems. Centrifugal pumps utilize rotational energy to move fluids, making them ideal for high flow rates and low viscosity applications. Turbine pumps, on the other hand, use a series of impellers to lift fluids, providing higher pressure and efficiency in demanding scenarios. Both types are crucial in industrial, agricultural, and municipal applications, offering reliable solutions for maintaining optimal pressure levels.</p></p>
