<p><ul><li>NDIR Sensors</li><li>Chemical Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The advanced gas sensors market encompasses two primary types: NDIR (Non-Dispersive Infrared) sensors and chemical sensors. NDIR sensors utilize infrared light to detect gas concentrations based on absorption characteristics, making them highly accurate and suitable for measuring gases like CO2 and CH4. Chemical sensors, on the other hand, rely on chemical reactions to detect the presence of specific gases, offering versatility in detecting various compounds, including hazardous gases. Both types are essential for environmental monitoring, safety applications, and industrial processes.</p></p>
