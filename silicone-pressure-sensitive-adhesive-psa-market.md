<p><strong>The Silicone Pressure Sensitive Adhesive (PSA) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Addition-cure</li><li>Peroxide-cure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Silicone Pressure Sensitive Adhesives (PSAs) are versatile adhesives that bond surfaces upon application of pressure without the need for heat or solvents. The market is segmented into addition-cure and peroxide-cure types. Addition-cure PSAs utilize platinum catalysts, resulting in low volatility and excellent thermal stability, ideal for high-performance applications. In contrast, peroxide-cure PSAs rely on organic peroxides for crosslinking, providing strong adhesion and versatility across various substrates. Both types serve diverse industries, including automotive, electronics, and medical.</p></p>
