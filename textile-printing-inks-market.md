<p><ul><li>Reactive Dye Inks</li><li>Acidic Ink</li><li>Paint Ink</li><li>Dispersion & Sublimation Ink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The textile printing inks market includes various types tailored for different fabrics and applications. **Reactive Dye Inks** are water-based, ideal for natural fibers like cotton, offering vibrant colors and durability. **Acidic Inks** are used for protein-based fabrics like silk and wool, ensuring excellent colorfastness. **Paint Inks** are pigment-based, suitable for all fabrics, providing opacity and versatility. **Dispersion Inks** are designed for synthetic fibers like polyester, offering strong adhesion. **Sublimation Inks** are heat-activated, transferring designs onto polyester with sharp, long-lasting prints. Each type caters to specific textile needs.</p></p>
