<p><p>Medium Density Fiberboard (MDF) panels are categorized into two main types for market purposes: Type I and Type II. Type I MDF is designed for interior applications, often featuring a smooth surface suitable for painting and veneering. It is commonly used in furniture and cabinetry. Type II MDF is moisture-resistant, making it ideal for environments with higher humidity, such as kitchens and bathrooms. These distinctions ensure that products meet specific performance standards for various applications in both residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
