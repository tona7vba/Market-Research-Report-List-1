<p><p>Visible Light Communications (VLC) utilizes light to transmit data, using LED lighting for high-speed connectivity. The market can be categorized into Access Points, which serve as the primary transmitters that convert data signals into light for communication, and Station Points, which receive the light signal and decode it back into data. Together, these components create a robust VLC network, enhancing applications in indoor navigation, wireless internet, and smart lighting systems, offering an alternative to traditional radio frequency communication methods.</p></p>
