<p><p>The Polypropylene (PP) foam market comprises three primary types of processing methods: extrusion molding, injection foaming molding, and compression molding. Extrusion molding involves continuous shaping of PP foam through a die, producing sheets or rolls. Injection foaming molding utilizes injected gas to create foam within a mold, allowing for intricate designs and shapes. Compression molding involves compressing heated PP material into a mold, resulting in dense, lightweight foam. Each method offers unique advantages for various applications in packaging, automotive, and construction industries.</p></p>
