<p><strong>The Instant Coffee Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spray-drying</li><li>Freeze-drying</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The instant coffee market is primarily categorized into two main types based on the drying process: spray-drying and freeze-drying. </p><p>Spray-drying involves rapidly evaporating water from coffee extract to create fine powder, resulting in a quick-dissolving product. It is typically more cost-effective and maintains a longer shelf life. </p><p>In contrast, freeze-drying preserves flavor and aroma by freezing the coffee extract and then removing moisture under vacuum conditions, yielding a higher-quality product that dissolves slower but offers a richer taste experience.</p></p>
