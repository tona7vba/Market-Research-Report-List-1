<p><p>In the optoelectronics market, adhesives are classified into three main types: liquid, solid, and paste. Liquid adhesives offer versatility and ease of application, enabling precise bonding in intricate electronic components. Solid adhesives, often in the form of films or strips, provide robust strength and durability for permanent fixtures. Paste adhesives, typically used for surface mounting or as a filler, offer excellent conformity and adhesion to various substrates. Each type is tailored to meet specific performance and application requirements in optoelectronic devices.</p></p>
