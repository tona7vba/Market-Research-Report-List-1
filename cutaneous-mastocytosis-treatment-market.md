<p><p>The Cutaneous Mastocytosis Treatment Market comprises several approaches. Antihistamines alleviate symptoms by blocking histamine receptors, while corticosteroids reduce inflammation and suppress immune responses. Mast cell stabilizers prevent mast cell degranulation, minimizing allergic reactions. Sympathomimetic agents, such as epinephrine, are crucial for treating severe allergic reactions by constricting blood vessels and dilating airways. Photochemotherapy employs light exposure combined with photosensitizing agents to manage skin lesions. Together, these treatments aim to control symptoms and improve the quality of life for affected patients.</p></p>
