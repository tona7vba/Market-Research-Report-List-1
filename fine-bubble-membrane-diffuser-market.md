<p><p>The Fine Bubble Membrane Diffuser market is primarily categorized into two types: disc diffusers and tube diffusers. Disc diffusers feature a flat, circular design that allows for efficient gas distribution in a broad area, making them suitable for larger applications. Tube diffusers, on the other hand, consist of elongated tubes that provide a linear distribution of fine bubbles, ideal for deeper tanks or specific layouts. Both types enhance aeration efficiency in wastewater treatment and aquaculture, improving oxygen transfer rates.</p></p>
