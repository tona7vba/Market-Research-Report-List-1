<p><p>The Silicon Carbide market is primarily categorized into three types based on their crystallographic structures: 3C-SiC (cubic), 4H-SiC (hexagonal), and 6H-SiC (hexagonal). 3C-SiC is favored for its compatibility with silicon technology, making it ideal for high-volume applications. 4H-SiC is known for its superior electrical properties, suited for high-power devices. 6H-SiC offers high thermal conductivity and is often used in high-temperature applications, catering to diverse industries such as automotive, aerospace, and telecommunications.</p></p>
