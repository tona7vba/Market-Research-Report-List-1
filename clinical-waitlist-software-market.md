<p><p>Clinical waitlist software is categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based software allows healthcare providers to access and manage waitlist data remotely via the internet, offering scalability, automatic updates, and reduced IT overhead. In contrast, on-premises software is installed locally on healthcare facilities' servers, providing greater control over data security and customization but requiring more maintenance and upfront costs. Each type caters to different organizational needs and operational strategies in managing patient waitlists effectively.</p></p>
