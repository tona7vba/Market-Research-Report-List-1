<p><strong>The Automotive OEM Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-Borne</li><li>Waterborne</li><li>Powdered</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive OEM coatings market comprises three main types: solvent-borne, waterborne, and powder coatings. Solvent-borne coatings utilize organic solvents for application, offering durability and excellent finish but with environmental concerns due to volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Waterborne coatings use water as the primary solvent, making them more eco-friendly while providing good performance and lower emissions. Powder coatings involve applying a dry powder that is cured under heat, resulting in a durable and resilient finish, ideal for various automotive applications.</p></p>
