<p><ul><li>Manifold Lyophilization Equipment</li><li>Rotary Lyophilization Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lyophilization equipment market includes various types, primarily focusing on manifold and rotary systems. Manifold lyophilization equipment features a compact design, allowing for efficient drying of small batches by connecting vials to a central vacuum system. In contrast, rotary lyophilization equipment utilizes rotating trays to enhance heat and mass transfer, ideal for larger volumes and continuous processing. Both types cater to different applications in pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and food preservation, ensuring optimized drying processes tailored to specific production needs.</p></p>
