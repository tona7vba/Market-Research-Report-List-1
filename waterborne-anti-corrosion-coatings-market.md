<p><p>Waterborne anti-corrosion coatings are specialized formulations primarily designed to protect metal surfaces from rust, corrosion, and degradation due to environmental factors. They find applications across various sectors such as automotive, construction, marine, and oil & gas, where corrosion prevention is critical.</p><p>The Waterborne Anti-corrosion Coatings Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.7% during the forecast period, driven by increasing awareness about environmental sustainability, stringent regulations on VOC emissions, and the growing demand for durable coatings in various industries. Innovations in polymer technology are also leading to improved adhesion, durability, and functionality.
