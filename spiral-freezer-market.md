<p><p>The spiral freezer market is primarily categorized into single drum and double drum types. Single drum spiral freezers feature one helix for product movement, offering efficient space usage and continuous freezing capabilities, making them ideal for smaller operations. In contrast, double drum spiral freezers utilize two interwoven helical paths, allowing for higher capacity and faster processing rates, suitable for larger production needs. Both types enhance product quality and minimize energy consumption, catering to diverse industrial applications in food preservation.</p></p>
