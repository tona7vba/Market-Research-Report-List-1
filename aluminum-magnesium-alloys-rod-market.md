<p><p>The Aluminum-Magnesium Alloys Rod market is categorized into Low Magnesium Alloys and High Magnesium Alloys. Low Magnesium Alloys typically contain 0.5% to 3% magnesium, offering moderate strength and good corrosion resistance, suitable for structural applications. In contrast, High Magnesium Alloys, with magnesium content over 3%, provide superior strength and enhanced weldability, making them ideal for demanding applications such as automotive and aerospace industries. Both types are valued for their lightweight properties and resistance to environmental degradation.</p></p>
