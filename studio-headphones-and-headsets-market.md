<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Studio Headphones and Headsets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Back</li><li>Semi-open Back</li><li>Fully-open Back</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Studio headphones and headsets are categorized into three main types based on their design: closed-back, semi-open back, and fully open-back. Closed-back headphones isolate sound, preventing external noise interference, making them ideal for tracking and mixing in noisy environments. Semi-open back offers a balance, providing some sound isolation while allowing a portion of ambient sound through, enhancing spatial awareness. Fully open-back headphones deliver a natural, immersive soundstage but lack isolation, making them suitable for critical listening in quiet settings.</p></p>
