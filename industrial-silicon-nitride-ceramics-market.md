<p><p>The Industrial Silicon Nitride Ceramics market is categorized into three main types: Reaction Sintered (RS), Gas Pressure Sintered (GPS), and Conventional Pressure Sintered (CPS) ceramics. RS ceramics are formed through a chemical reaction, resulting in high strength and stability. GPS ceramics are produced under controlled gas pressure, enhancing their density and toughness, making them suitable for demanding applications. CPS ceramics, which are sintered under conventional pressure, offer a balance of performance and cost, catering to various industrial needs.</p></p>
