<p><p>Die Attach Film (DAF) is a specialized adhesive material used in semiconductor packaging to bond semiconductor dies to substrates or lead frames. The miniaturization trend in electronics and the shift toward advanced packaging solutions like fan-out wafer-level packaging (FOWLP) are further propelling market expansion.</p><p>Key trends in the Die Attach Film Market include the development of low-temperature curing films for heat-sensitive components, the integration of DAF in 5G and IoT devices, and the growing use of eco-friendly materials. Asia-Pacific remains a dominant market due to its strong semiconductor manufacturing base, while North America and Europe are witnessing steady growth driven by technological advancements and innovation in electronics.</p></p>
