<p><p>The inflatable penile prosthesis market includes three main types: 3-piece, semi-inflatable, and semi-rigid. The 3-piece model consists of two cylinders, a reservoir, and a pump, offering a natural erection appearance and enhanced control over rigidity. Semi-inflatable devices have a simpler design that allows for easier activation while maintaining a degree of rigidity. Semi-rigid prostheses are malleable rods that provide continuous firmness but lack the adjustability of the inflatable options, catering to different patient preferences and needs.</p></p>
