<p><ul><li>Immersion Types</li><li>Water Bath Types</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sous vide cooking machine market is primarily divided into immersion types and water bath types. Immersion sous vide machines feature a heating element and circulator that can be clipped onto any pot, allowing precise temperature control. Water bath types are standalone units with built-in water reservoirs, ensuring consistent heating and circulation for optimal cooking results. Both types enable sous vide cooking, providing excellent texture and flavor retention in food, catering to home cooks and professional kitchens alike.</p></p>
