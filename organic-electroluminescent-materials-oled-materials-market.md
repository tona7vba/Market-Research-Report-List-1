<p><ul><li>Emission Layer Materials</li><li>Common Layer Materials</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Organic Electroluminescent Materials (OLED Materials) are integral to display and lighting technologies, generating light through organic compounds. The market is categorized into emission layer materials, which produce light when electricity is applied, and common layer materials, which facilitate charge transport and enhance efficiency. Emission layer materials include small molecules and polymers that emit specific colors, while common layer materials typically involve hole transport and electron transport layers. These components work together to improve performance and longevity in OLED devices.</p></p>
