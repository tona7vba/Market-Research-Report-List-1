<p><p>Die attach adhesives for the semiconductor market come in three main types: insulating, sintering, and heat-curing. Insulating adhesives provide electrical insulation and thermal stability, essential for reliable semiconductor performance. Sintering adhesives utilize heat and pressure to achieve strong bonds, often enhancing thermal conductivity. Heat-curing adhesives require elevated temperatures to cure and create durable connections. Each type serves specific applications in semiconductor packaging, ensuring optimal functionality and reliability in electronic devices.</p></p>
