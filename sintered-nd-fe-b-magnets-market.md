<p><p>The sintered Nd-Fe-B magnets market is categorized mainly into axially magnetized and diametrically magnetized types. Axially magnetized magnets have their magnetic poles oriented along the axis, making them ideal for applications in motors and generators where uniform magnetic fields are essential. Diametrically magnetized magnets, on the other hand, have their poles arranged around the diameter, suitable for rotary applications such as sensors and wireless power transfer. Both types serve unique applications across various industries, driving their market demand.</p></p>
