Protease is essential for protein digestion, helping to convert proteins into amino acids, which are vital for growth and repair. Amylase facilitates carbohydrate digestion, transforming polysaccharides into simpler sugars for energy. Together, these enzymes enhance pets' digestive health, support overall well-being, and contribute to better nutrient utilization from their diets.</p></p>
