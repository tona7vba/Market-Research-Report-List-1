<p><p>The untempered steel market comprises three main types: ferrite-pearlite steel, bainite steel, and martensitic steel. Ferrite-pearlite steel, characterized by its balanced ductility and strength, is widely used in construction. Bainite steel, known for its toughness, offers excellent wear resistance, making it suitable for automotive applications. Martensitic steel, recognized for its high hardness and strength, is ideal for tools and heavy-duty applications. Each type serves specific industrial needs, contributing to the diverse untempered steel market.</p></p>
