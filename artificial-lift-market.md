<p><p>The artificial lift market comprises several technologies designed to enhance oil and gas production. Rod lift employs a mechanical rod to raise fluid, while electric submersible pumps (ESP) utilize electric power to facilitate extraction. Progressive cavity pumps (PCP) use a rotating helical screw, hydraulic pumps apply pressure to lift fluids, and gas lift injects gas to reduce fluid density. Plunger lift uses a moving plunger to lift liquids, and other methods encompass emerging and specialized technologies for specific production needs.</p></p>
