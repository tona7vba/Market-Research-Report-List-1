<p><p>The water-filtration unit market comprises various types, each serving specific purification needs. Bag and cartridge filters capture larger particles and sediments, while activated carbon filters eliminate chlorine and odors. Media filters utilize layered materials to trap impurities, and sediment filters target fine particles for clearer water. Reverse osmosis filters are advanced systems that remove dissolved solids and contaminants through a semi-permeable membrane, providing high-quality drinking water. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing water quality for residential and commercial use.</p></p>
