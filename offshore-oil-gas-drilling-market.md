<p><p>The offshore oil and gas drilling market comprises three primary types: jackups, semi-submersibles, and drillships. Jackups are mobile platforms with legs that elevate above the water, ideal for shallow waters. Semi-submersibles are floating vessels anchored to the seabed, providing stability in deeper waters and harsh environments. Drillships are advanced floating rigs equipped with dynamic positioning systems, capable of operating in very deep waters. Each type serves distinct operational needs based on water depth and environmental conditions.</p></p>
