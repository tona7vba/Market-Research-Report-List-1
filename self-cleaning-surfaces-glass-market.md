<p><p>The self-cleaning surfaces glass market is characterized by two primary types: hydrophilic and hydrophobic. Hydrophilic surfaces attract water, allowing droplets to spread and wash away dirt and contaminants, facilitating easier cleaning. In contrast, hydrophobic surfaces repel water, causing it to bead and roll off, taking dirt with it. Both types enhance maintenance efficiency and are popular in various applications, including buildings, automotive, and consumer products, offering innovative solutions for cleanliness and durability in diverse environments.</p></p>
