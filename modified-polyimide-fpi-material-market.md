<p><p>The Modified Polyimide (FPI) material market encompasses two primary types: MPI resin and MPI film. MPI resin is known for its excellent thermal stability, chemical resistance, and mechanical properties, making it suitable for a variety of applications in electronics, aerospace, and automotive sectors. MPI film, on the other hand, offers flexible and lightweight solutions with high dielectric strength and resistance to extreme temperatures, ideal for insulation and protective applications in advanced technologies. Both types play crucial roles in modern manufacturing.</p></p>
