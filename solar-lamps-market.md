<p><p>The solar lamps market primarily includes two main types: Compact Fluorescent Lamps (CFLs) and Light Emitting Diodes (LEDs). CFLs are energy-efficient and provide good illumination, making them suitable for various outdoor settings. They consume less energy than traditional bulbs but are less durable than LEDs. Conversely, LEDs are highly energy-efficient, have a longer lifespan, and emit bright light, making them ideal for solar applications. The growing demand for sustainable lighting solutions drives the adoption of both lamp types globally.</p></p>
