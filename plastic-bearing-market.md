<p><strong>The Plastic Bearing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plastic Rolling Bearings</li><li>Plastic Sliding Bearings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic bearing market comprises two primary types: plastic rolling bearings and plastic sliding bearings. Plastic rolling bearings utilize rolling elements, such as balls or rollers, made from lightweight thermoplastics, offering low friction and reduced weight, making them ideal for applications in corrosive environments. Plastic sliding bearings, on the other hand, rely on a sliding motion and typically incorporate materials with self-lubricating properties, suitable for applications requiring low maintenance and high durability, often in machinery and automotive sectors.</p></p>
