<p><p>Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy Defibrillator (CRT-D) is an advanced cardiac device designed to treat heart failure patients by coordinating the heart's contractions and providing defibrillation capabilities to manage arrhythmias. These devices are implanted in patients with heart rhythm disorders and help improve the heart's efficiency, enhance quality of life, and reduce hospitalizations. The integration of defibrillation technology allows CRT-Ds to deliver lifesaving shocks if dangerous arrhythmias occur.</p><p>The Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy Defibrillator Market is expected to witness significant growth, driven by an increasing prevalence of heart diseases, a growing aging population, and advancements in technology.
