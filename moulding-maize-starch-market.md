<p><p>The moulding maize starch market is categorized into three types based on digestibility. Rapidly digestible starch is quickly broken down by the body, providing immediate energy, making it ideal for sports nutrition. Slowly digestible starch is digested at a slower rate, offering a sustained energy release, suitable for health-focused products. Resistant starch, which bypasses digestion in the small intestine, promotes digestive health and can aid in weight management. Together, these starch types cater to diverse consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
