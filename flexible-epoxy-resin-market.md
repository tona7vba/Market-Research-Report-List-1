<p><p>The flexible epoxy resin market encompasses various types, including urethane modified, rubber modified, and dimer acid formulations. Urethane modified epoxy resins enhance flexibility and impact resistance, making them ideal for coatings and adhesives. Dimer acid epoxy resins offer improved adhesion and chemical resistance, often used in industrial applications.
