<p><p>The livestock dewormers market comprises three primary types: topical, injection, and oral formulations. Topical dewormers are applied directly to the skin, providing localized treatment and ease of use. Injection dewormers are administered via syringe, ensuring quick absorption and systemic effectiveness against internal parasites. Oral dewormers are delivered through feed or water, making them convenient for livestock management. Each type offers distinct advantages in terms of application, efficacy, and target parasites, catering to varying needs within the livestock industry.</p></p>
