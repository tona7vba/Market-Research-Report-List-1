<p><p>The label adhesives market comprises three main types: water-based, hot melt-based, and solvent-based adhesives. Water-based adhesives are eco-friendly, utilizing water as a solvent, making them suitable for various substrates. Hot melt-based adhesives provide strong bonds and quick setting times, ideal for high-speed production. Solvent-based adhesives offer excellent adhesion and durability, especially for challenging surfaces, but may emit volatile organic compounds. Each type caters to different application needs, balancing performance, environmental impact, and production efficiency.</p></p>
