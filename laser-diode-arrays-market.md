<p><strong>The Laser Diode Arrays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Microchannel Cooled</li><li>Conductively Cooled</li><li>Fluid Cooled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laser diode arrays are categorized into three main cooling types: microchannel cooled, conductively cooled, and fluid cooled. Microchannel cooled arrays utilize small channels to efficiently dissipate heat through a cooling fluid, maximizing performance. Conductively cooled systems rely on solid materials to transfer heat away from the diode, offering simplicity and compact design. Fluid cooled arrays employ liquid cooling systems to manage thermal output, suitable for high-power applications where substantial heat generation occurs. Each type serves specific operational needs within the market.</p></p>
