<p><p>A Motorcycle Inertial Measurement Unit (IMU) Sensor is a device that measures and reports a motorcycle's specific force, angular rate, and orientation using a combination of accelerometers, gyroscopes, and sometimes magnetometers. The market is expected to grow at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 9.2% during the forecast period. This growth is fueled by stricter safety regulations, consumer preference for high-performance motorcycles, and the integration of IMU sensors in mid-range and premium motorcycles.</p><p>Latest trends in the market include the development of compact, lightweight, and cost-effective IMU sensors, as well as the integration of artificial intelligence and machine learning for predictive analytics. Additionally, the rise of connected motorcycles and the Internet of Things (IoT) is creating opportunities for enhanced data collection and analysis, further driving market expansion. The Asia-Pacific region, particularly India and China, is expected to dominate the market due to high motorcycle sales and increasing safety awareness.</p></p>
