<p><ul><li>Single-layer Interference Coating</li><li>Multi-layer Interference Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anti-reflectant coating market is primarily categorized into single-layer interference coatings and multi-layer interference coatings. Single-layer coatings consist of a single film applied to a substrate, effectively reducing reflection for specific wavelengths of light. In contrast, multi-layer coatings involve several layers of materials, each designed to target different wavelengths, resulting in enhanced performance across a broader spectrum. This differentiation allows for tailored solutions in various applications, including optical devices, cameras, and solar panels, improving light transmission and overall efficiency.</p></p>
