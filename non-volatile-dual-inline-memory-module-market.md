<p><p>The Non-Volatile Dual In-Line Memory Module (NVDIMM) market consists of two primary types: NVDIMM-N and NVDIMM-F. NVDIMM-N combines traditional DRAM with NAND flash memory, providing fast data access while retaining information during power loss. NVDIMM-F, on the other hand, relies solely on NAND flash memory, offering greater capacity and cost-efficiency for applications needing higher data retention without DRAM's speed advantages. Both types cater to enterprises requiring robust data storage solutions for critical applications and high-performance computing environments.</p></p>
