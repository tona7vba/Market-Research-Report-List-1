<p><ul><li>CSF Shunts</li><li>CSF Drainage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cerebral Spinal Fluid (CSF) management market is primarily divided into two main types: CSF shunts and CSF drainage systems. CSF shunts are devices used to redirect excess cerebrospinal fluid from the brain to another part of the body, helping to treat conditions like hydrocephalus. In contrast, CSF drainage systems are designed to facilitate the removal of excess fluid, often utilized in acute situations to relieve pressure. Both types play crucial roles in managing neurological disorders effectively.</p></p>
