<p><strong>The Steel Wire Rope Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Left Regular Lay</li><li>Left Lang Lay</li><li>Right Regular Lay</li><li>Right Lang Lay</li><li>Alternate Lay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steel wire rope market features various lay types, each defined by the direction and pattern of the wires. Left Regular Lay has wires twisted to the left in a regular pattern, while Left Lang Lay features left-twisted wires laid in a longer pitch. Conversely, Right Regular Lay has wires twisted to the right, and Right Lang Lay consists of right-twisted wires in a longer pitch. Alternate Lay combines both right and left twists, enhancing flexibility and strength, catering to different industrial applications.</p></p>
