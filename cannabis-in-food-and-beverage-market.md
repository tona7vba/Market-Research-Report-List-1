Marijuana-derived products contain higher THC levels, providing psychoactive effects, and are typically found in recreational or medicinal consumables. Synthetic cannabis refers to lab-created compounds designed to mimic the effects of natural cannabis, offering a controlled alternative for consumers seeking specific experiences or effects.</p></p>
