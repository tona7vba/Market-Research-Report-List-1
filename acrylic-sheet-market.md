<p><strong>The Acrylic Sheet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cast Acrylic Sheet</li><li>Extruded Acrylic Sheet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The acrylic sheet market primarily consists of two types: cast acrylic sheets and extruded acrylic sheets. Cast acrylic sheets are produced by polymerizing liquid acrylic between two glass plates, resulting in a thicker and more durable product, ideal for applications requiring clarity and impact resistance. In contrast, extruded acrylic sheets are formed by melting acrylic pellets and forcing them through a mold, leading to thinner sheets that are easier to work with and often more cost-effective for various applications.</p></p>
