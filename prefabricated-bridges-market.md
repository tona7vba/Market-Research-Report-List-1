<p><p>The **Prefabricated Bridges Market** includes various types designed for specific applications. **Beam Type** bridges use horizontal beams supported by piers, ideal for short to medium spans. **Arch Type** bridges feature curved structures that distribute weight efficiently, suitable for longer spans and aesthetic appeal. **Suspension Type** bridges utilize cables and towers for support, perfect for very long spans and challenging terrains. **Others** include truss, cable-stayed, and movable bridges, catering to unique engineering needs and site-specific requirements. Each type offers distinct advantages in durability, cost, and construction efficiency.</p></p>
