<p><p>RF transformers are categorized into three main market types based on frequency range: low frequency, medium frequency, and high frequency. Low frequency transformers operate typically below 1 MHz, supporting applications like audio transmission. Medium frequency transformers, ranging from 1 MHz to 30 MHz, are commonly used in radio broadcasting and communications. High frequency transformers, exceeding 30 MHz, find applications in advanced communication systems, RF amplifiers, and wireless technologies, catering to the increasing demand for efficient signal processing and transmission.</p></p>
