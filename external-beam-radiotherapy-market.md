<p><p>The External Beam Radiotherapy market encompasses various advanced techniques for delivering precise radiation treatment. Intensity-modulated Radiotherapy (IMRT) optimizes dose distribution to target tumors while sparing surrounding tissue. Image-guided Radiotherapy (IGRT) enhances accuracy through real-time imaging. TomoTherapy combines radiation delivery with CT scanning for precise targeting. Stereotactic Therapy focuses high doses on small tumors. 3D Conformal Radiotherapy shapes radiation to the tumor's geometry. Particle Beam Therapy utilizes protons or heavier ions for targeted treatment, while Volumetric Modulated Arc Therapy offers continuous dose modulation in a single rotation.</p></p>
