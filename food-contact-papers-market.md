<p><p>The Food Contact Papers market is segmented into two main types: Poly Coated Paper and Non-Poly Coated Paper. Poly Coated Paper features a layer of polyethylene that provides moisture resistance and enhances durability, making it ideal for packaging fatty or wet food items. In contrast, Non-Poly Coated Paper lacks this coating, offering a more eco-friendly option; it is typically used for dry foods and products that do not require a barrier against moisture. Both types cater to diverse packaging needs in the food industry.</p></p>
