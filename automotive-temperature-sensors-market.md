<p><p>The automotive temperature sensors market features various technologies to monitor vehicle temperatures. Thermistors provide rapid response and high accuracy. Resistance Temperature Detectors (RTDs) offer stability over a wide range but are typically bulkier. Thermocouples are cost-effective and suitable for extreme environments. Integrated Circuit (IC) temperature sensors deliver compact solutions for electronic applications. MEMS (Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems) sensors provide miniaturization and precision, while infrared sensors enable non-contact measurement, enhancing safety and efficiency in modern automotive systems.</p></p>
