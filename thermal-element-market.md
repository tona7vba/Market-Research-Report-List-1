<p><p>The thermal element market is divided into two main types: positive temperature and negative temperature markets. Conversely, negative temperature sensors, often based on thermistors, are utilized to measure lower temperatures or monitor cooling applications. Both markets cater to specific applications, with their distinct temperature ranges and materials, ensuring accurate temperature monitoring and control across various industries, from manufacturing to environmental monitoring.</p></p>
