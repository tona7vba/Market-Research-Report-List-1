<p><p>Vane motors are categorized into pneumatic and hydraulic types based on their operating mediums. Pneumatic vane motors utilize compressed air to generate rotary motion, making them ideal for lightweight and portable applications. They offer high speed and low maintenance. In contrast, hydraulic vane motors operate using pressurized fluids, providing greater torque and power for heavy-duty tasks. Their robust design suits industrial environments where high force and durability are essential, facilitating efficient performance in various machinery and applications.</p></p>
